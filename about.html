About Our School

Established in 1989, Little Star High School has been a beacon of quality education and character development in Tengakhat, Dibrugarh. Over the decades, our school has nurtured thousands of students, empowering them with knowledge, confidence, and values that shape a brighter future.

We are committed to providing a safe, supportive, and inspiring learning environment where every student can reach their full potential. Our dedicated teachers focus on both academic excellence and personal growth, ensuring that every child becomes a confident and responsible global citizen.
